Motor Selection

Choosing the right motor is crucial for ensuring optimal performance and efficiency of your drone. The KV rating of a motor defines the RPM per volt, which directly impacts the drone's speed and stability.

Steps to Determine KV Rating:

  1. Calculate the total weight of the drone.
  2. Determine the thrust required for stable flight.
  3. Select propeller size based on the desired thrust and efficiency.
  4. Use an online motor calculator to input the weight, propeller size, and battery voltage to get the recommended KV rating.

For our carbon fiber drone, after inputting the necessary parameters, we found that a motor with a 750 KV rating is optimal.

Formulas

Here are the key formulas used in the motor selection process:

  • Thrust Calculation:
    Thrust per Motor = (Total Weight × 2) / Number of Motors
  • Power Calculation:
    Power = Voltage × Current
  • KV Rating Relation:
    Motor RPM = KV × Voltage
